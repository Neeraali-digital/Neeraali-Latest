Introduction

Welcome to Neeraali Digital Solutions ("Neeraali Digital," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website or use our services.

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or our practices, please contact us at connect@neeraali.com.

By using our website https://www.neeraali.com (the "Website") and our services, you agree to this Privacy Policy and consent to our data handling practices described herein.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal information to provide better services and improve user experience. This includes:

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Company details and project information
  • Communication preferences and feedback
  • Payment and billing details (processed securely via third-party gateways)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Usage patterns, pages visited, and referral sources
  • Device and location information
  • Cookies and similar technologies

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Social media platforms (LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram)
  • Analytics tools like Google Analytics
  • Payment processors and CRM systems

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Provide and manage our services
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve user experience and website functionality
  • Send updates, offers, and newsletters (only with consent)
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations

3. SHARING YOUR INFORMATION

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, payment, or email marketing partners
  • Business Partners: For collaborative marketing or project purposes (with your consent)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or regulation

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use cookies to:

  • Maintain session information
  • Analyze website traffic and performance
  • Personalize your experience

You can control or delete cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit some functionality.

5. DATA SECURITY

We apply strong security measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Firewalls and secure servers
  • Access control and data backups
  • Regular audits and employee training

While we strive for full security, no method of transmission is 100% secure, and users should take precautions with their credentials.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain data only as long as needed for legitimate business or legal purposes. Once data is no longer necessary, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

You may:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Object to certain types of data processing

Requests can be made via connect@neeraali.com. We respond within 30 days.
